Tired of asking “what’s for dinner?” Meal Planner turns the dishes your household already cooks into a shared family menu. People pick what they want. The cook sees one clear order. The grocery list comes together before anyone heads to the store.

A menu built from your real home cooking
Save the dishes your family actually repeats, from weeknight staples to weekend favorites. Add a name, category, ingredients, and an optional photo so the menu feels familiar instead of generic.

Meal orders everyone can see
Family members choose dishes from the shared menu, and Meal Planner combines their picks into the current order. The cook can move each dish through the cooking flow, so everyone knows what is waiting, what is cooking, and what is done.

A grocery list from the active meal
Before shopping, open the grocery list generated from the current order. It gathers the ingredients you need in one place, helping you avoid forgotten items and last-minute messages.

Private family kitchens
Create a kitchen, invite members by code, and keep meal planning inside the people you cook for. Roles help separate menu management from everyday ordering, and finished meals are saved to history for easy reference.

Free to start
The free tier supports up to 10 dishes. One-time purchases unlock 50 dishes or unlimited dishes for a kitchen. No subscriptions, no ads, and no third-party tracking SDKs.
